Intro

Hi! I'm Jack, and I'm a MCDB graduate from the University of Michigan. My passion for the subject began in high school where I stumbled upon a couple of biology books which blew my mind. This passion led me to compete in the British Biology olympiad, where I won a Gold.
Since then, I've continued pursuing biology in Michigan, where I've had the pleasure to explore a wide range of classes such as biochemistry, genetics, and even a graduate class in immunology. AT university, I have also thorougly delved into research in many areas, and have since narrowed my focus on structural biology. I am captivated by uncovering the mysterious relationship between the structure of macromolecules and their function, as well as the freedom to exercise creativity in manipulating them for our benefit. I currently work as a tech at the Mosalaganti lab, where I work on various structure related projects. In the future, I intend to pursue graduate school, where I can continue deepening my understanding of structural biology.
